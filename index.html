Beat-Making Classes Schedule

Hello! I'm Charmer, and I'll be hosting beat-making classes for complete beginners. No prior experience is required to join, and the only things you'll need are a laptop and headphones. We'll cover everything from the basics of beat-making to creating full songs step-by-step. Below is the schedule for our classes.

Each session will be held live for 1.5 hours, and no prior experience is required.To join, or for more information, reach out to me via WhatsApp at +254795307358
