Beat-Making Classes Schedule

Hello! I'm Charmer, and I'll be teaching my girl how to make beats next year.
I have considered teaching her alongside anyone else who is interested.
No prior experience is required to join, and the only things you'll need are a laptop and headphones. We'll cover everything from the basics of beat-making to creating full songs step-by-step. Below is the schedule for our classes.
Each session will be held live for 1.5 hours. To join, or for more information, reach out to me via WhatsApp at +254795307358.
